About Us

Welcome to Sahasra Hospital

A beacon of healthcare excellence in Adilabad and surrounding regions

Sahasra Hospital is a premier multi-specialty healthcare institution located in the heart of Adilabad, Telangana. We are committed to delivering world-class medical care with compassion, integrity, and clinical precision.

Our team of highly qualified specialists brings together decades of experience in Pulmonary Medicine, Obstetrics & Gynecology, and General Consultation. Every patient who walks through our doors is treated like family — with personalized attention and evidence-based treatment plans.

We combine modern diagnostic technology with the warmth of traditional Indian hospitality to create a healing environment that promotes faster recovery and lasting wellness.
